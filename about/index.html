About

I earned my BA from Moi University. I’m a Geospatial Analyst working at International Center for Tropical Agriculture (CIAT). Prior to that, I worked at African Biodiversity Conservation and Innovations Centre, Kenya Electricity Generating Company (KENGEN), Department of Resource Surveys and Remote Sensing (DRSRS) and Kenya Meteorological Department (KMD). I have consulted for French Research Institute for Development and VACID Africa among other organisations.

My work revolves around creating reproducible workflows for analyzing complex spatial data, with appropriate analytical methods, informed by my unique combination of skills, experience, and understanding of research themes - climate change, food security and agriculture. I have contributed to the development of multiple data analysis workflows and tools such as Targeting Tools, Global Soil Data Manager - Sub-Saharan Africa. I have written numerous scientific publications on the above subjects. More about my work is on GitHub.

My CV is available here, and if you’re curious about how I TeXed it up, view the code. Please feel free to contact me if you have exciting suggestions or questions!
